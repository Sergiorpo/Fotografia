Sergio Rodríguez

Hi there! My name is Sergio, and I am a amateur photographer based in Madrid, Spain. Photography has been my passion for many years.

I have studied photography , both digital and analog, I also have knowledge of graphic design and have studied computer science, which I have been working in for some time.

My technical expertise allows me to create beautiful and thought-provoking images. I am always striving to improve my skills and push the boundaries of my artistic vision.

I enjoy street, landscape, and Photoshop composite photography. I believe these genres allow me to capture the world in a unique and striking way.

Thank you for visiting my photography website. I hope you enjoy my work. I am always open to new ideas and collaborations, so please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or suggestions. I look forward to hearing from you!
